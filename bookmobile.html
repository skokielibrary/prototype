Bookmobile

Our Bookmobile is a mobile library that brings books, DVDs, and more to week. You can use your library card to check out and return items just as you would inside the library building. Bookmobile for you, call 847-673-7774 at least one day in advance.

For more information on Bookmobile services, email Gail Dunlap at gdunlap@skokielibrary.info.
